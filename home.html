About Us

Volunteer for Lebanon is a non-profit, non-sectarian, non-governmental organization whose mission is to promote, facilitate and improve volunteering and community service throughout Lebanon. Much of volunteering in Lebanon is done by individuals who support the programs and operations of organizations and institutions. But increasingly, youth groups, school classes, clubs, and corporate volunteer programs want to do service projects and need the infrastructure to facilitate their efforts. Projects for Lebanon is a web-based platform to provide that infrastructure.

Ways To Get Involved

I'm an individual

    As individuals, you can serve as:
  • The contact person for a group service project
  • A volunteer for a group project that still needs volunteers
  • A mentor who uses your special skills to help a group do a service project
  • A teacher, adult leader of a youth group, or coordinator of a corporate volunteer program who inspires, initiates, and/or coordinates a service project
  • Donor of in-kind and financial support

Sign up

I'm an organization

    As NGOs, service institutions, schools, universities... you can:
  • Propose projects that groups can do for your non-profit institution
  • Develop and implement group service projects for other institutions
  • Provide mentors for projects
  • Provide in-kind and financial support for projects
  • Provide facilities for groups to plan and/or implement their projects


Join

As partners

    As government agencies, companies, foundations, and grant-awarding institutions, you can:
  • Serve as sponsors for the Service Project Support Program or Volunteer for Lebanon in general
  • Fund a micro-grant program administered by Volunteer for Lebanon to support youth service projects
  • Fund a tool-share project of Volunteer for Lebanon to loan tools to groups doing service projects
