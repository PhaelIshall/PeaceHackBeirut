Operation Big Blue

Beach cleanup

organizes campaigns to clean the beaches of Lebanon. Individual groups through out Lebanon can select a beach to clean, and arrange for the sorting of the garbage and the recycling of the bottles and cans that are collected. Coordinate with us which beach you want to be responsible for, and we will provide T-shirts, caps, and bags to put the waste in.
